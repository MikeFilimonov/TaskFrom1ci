Bank accounts

1C:Drive stores bank accounts of companies and individuals in the Bank accounts list.

The Bank accounts list is available for each item in the Companies, Counterparties, and Individuals lists.

If an organization has several bank accounts, you can mark one of them as the main bank account. To do this, select a bank account and click Use as the main one. As a result, 1C:Drive automatically enters the account into payment documents.

For each bank account, 1C:Drive provides analytical accounting of cash assets.

A bank account has the following fields:

  • Account holder. The company that owns the account.
  • Account kind. The account purpose: current, deposit, loan, or other.
  • Account number. The bank account number in national format.
  • Currency. The account currency. It is selected from the Currencies list.
  • IBAN. The bank account number in IBAN (International Bank Account Number) format.
  • Bank
    • SWIFT. The bank ID in SWIFT format.
    • Bank. The bank where the account was opened.
    • City. The bank city.
    • Presentation. The account name in account lists. Typically, it is the account number or IBAN.
    • Payments are conducted via correspondent account opened in other bank (settlement bank). Defines whether the bank that serves the account uses a correspondent account in another bank (settlement bank) for transfers. If you select this check box, specify the settlement bank details in the following fields:
      • SWIFT. The settlement bank's ID in SWIFT format.
      • Bank. The settlement bank.
      • City. The settlement bank city.
  • Print settings
    • Edit company name text. Defines whether the company name is editable in Company name in "Payer" field.
    • Company name in "Payer" field. The company name for adding to the Payer field in payment documents.
    • Month display option. Month output format: in words or as a number.
    • Display an amount without cents if value is without cents. If you select the check box, the payment document amount is displayed with the "=" symbol and without cents. Otherwise, the "-" decimal separator is used and cents are displayed. For example, the 40 dollar amount is displayed as 40= and 40-00 respectively.
    • Edit counterparty (individual) name. Defines whether counterparty's (or individual's) name is editable in Counterparty (individual) name text in the "Recipient" field.
    • Counterparty (individual) name text in the "Recipient" field. The counterparty or individual name for adding to the Recipient field in payment documents.
    • Payment purpose text. The payment purpose text for adding to payment documents.
  • Additional information
    • Opening date. The date when the bank account was opened.
    • Closing date. The date when the bank account was closed.
  • GL accounts. For the bank account, select an appropriate GL account from the Primary chart of accounts chart of accounts. This field is required for your company's bank accounts. For bank accounts of your counterparties and individuals, the field is optional.
