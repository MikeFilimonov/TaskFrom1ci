Earning and deduction kinds catalog

The catalog contains a list of all employee Earning and deduction information required for payroll.

 

You can group kinds of Earnings and deductions according to the catalog classifier groups, e.g. "Earning" and "Deduction".

You can edit the Earning and deduction information in a separate dialog box opened in the catalog list form.

 The following information is to be specified:

  • Type. Either Earning or deduction.
  • GL account. The default GL account for accrued payroll.
  • Tax kind. Tax kind used for the tax Earning.
  • Formula. Formula used to calculate amounts of Earnings and deductions. The formula is edited in a separate dialog box. The formula may include the elements of the Earnings calculation parameters catalog specified at the bottom of the form, numeric characters, characters (+, -, *, /) and functions (? (), Min (), Max (), Round (), etc.) Select the calculations parameter by double-clicking it. To view and edit parameters, click Edit (or open) current item on the command bar or press F4.
