Product characteristics catalog

In the catalog, you can view information about additional characteristics of products items (color, size, etc.) These characteristics are used for inventory accounting.

The catalog is used if the Accounting by characteristics system parameter is selected. The parameter value is defined in the Settings - Accounting settings section.

You can edit the product characteristics from the form of the catalog list opened from the form of the Products or Group of products catalog item.

Characteristics are assigned only for the items having the Accounting by characteristics check box selected.

The products item descriptions contain a list of values of specific property kinds. Different property kinds may be defined for different product groups.

For example, to describe characteristics of products from the Shoes group, you can use such property kinds as size, color, width. For products of the Fridges group, use such property kinds as dimensions, freezer size and color.

Define property kinds in the Additional attributes and data characteristic kind plan.

When you select specific products, inventory balance is shown in terms of different characteristics. You can add a new characteristic for a specific products item when you create the document, e.g. when inventory is delivered to the warehouse.
