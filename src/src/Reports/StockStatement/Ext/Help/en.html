Inventory report

You can use the report to get the complete data on the receipt, shipment and current available balance of inventory, as well as inventory amount reserved for sales orders. The report contains financial and managerial state of inventory, and data for the manager. In addition, the data is grouped by warehouses and suppliers. If data is grouped by suppliers, you see the last supplier for which the price is registered as of the end date of the report period.

The report contains data on initial and closing balances at the company’s and counterparties’ warehouses. The report data is presented in quantity and totals. 
 
The report can be generated in two modes – Statement and Balances. IN the Balances mode the data on receipts and expenses is not shown in the report, you can see the final balances only.
